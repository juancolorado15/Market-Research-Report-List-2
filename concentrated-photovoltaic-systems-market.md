<p><p>Concentrated Photovoltaic Systems market consists of two main types: High Concentrated Photovoltaic (HCPV) and Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV). HCPV systems use lenses or mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a small area of high-efficiency solar cells, resulting in higher electricity production per unit area. On the other hand, LCPV systems use less intense concentration, allowing for a wider range of locations and lower costs. Both types offer advantages in terms of energy production efficiency and cost-effectiveness, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
