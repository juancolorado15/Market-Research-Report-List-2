<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Deaf Aid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIC</li><li>ITC</li><li>ITE</li><li>BTE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deaf Aid market can be categorized into different types based on the style and placement of hearing aids. Completely-in-the-canal (CIC) hearing aids are small and discreet, fitting entirely inside the ear canal. In-the-canal (ITC) hearing aids are slightly larger and more visible, resting partly in the ear canal. In-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids are custom-made to fit in the outer ear. Behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids sit behind the ear and are connected to a custom earpiece that fits inside the ear canal.</p></p>
