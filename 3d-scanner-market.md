<p><strong>The 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser 3D Scanner</li><li>Structured Light 3D Scanner</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D scanner market can be divided into different types based on the technology used. Laser 3D scanners use laser beams to capture the shape of objects with high precision. Structured light 3D scanners project a pattern of light onto the object and use the distortion of the pattern to create a 3D image. Other types of 3D scanners may use technologies such as time-of-flight or photogrammetry. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in industries like manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment.</p></p>
