<p><ul><li>Formed tube Cold Plate</li><li>Deep drilled Cold Plate</li><li>Machined channel Cold Plates</li><li>Pocketed folded-fin Cold Plates</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Plate market types include Formed tube Cold Plate, which are made by bending and welding tubes to create a cooling channel. Deep drilled Cold Plates are manufactured by drilling deep holes for the coolant to pass through. Machined channel Cold Plates have channels machined into a solid metal block for efficient cooling. Pocketed folded-fin Cold Plates feature folded fins that create more surface area for heat dissipation. The Others market category includes unique designs and materials that do not fit into the other types.</p></p>
