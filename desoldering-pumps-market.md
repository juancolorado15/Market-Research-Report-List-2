<p><p>Desoldering pumps come in two main types - Plunger type and Bulb type. Plunger type desoldering pumps have a spring-loaded plunger mechanism that creates suction when pressed down, making it easy to remove solder from electronic components. On the other hand, Bulb type desoldering pumps have a rubber bulb that is squeezed to create suction for removing solder. Both types are commonly used in electronics repair and soldering work, with each having its own advantages and preferences among users.</p></p>
