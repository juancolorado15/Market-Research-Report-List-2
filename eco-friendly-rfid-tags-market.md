<p><strong>The Eco-Friendly RFID Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li><li>Semi-Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Eco-friendly RFID tags are commonly classified into three types: active, passive, and semi-passive. Active RFID tags have a power source and can transmit signals over long distances, making them suitable for tracking large items. Passive RFID tags do not have a power source and rely on energy from the RFID reader to transmit information, making them cost-effective and ideal for tracking smaller items. Semi-passive RFID tags have a power source but rely on the reader for data transmission, striking a balance between active and passive tags.</p></p>
