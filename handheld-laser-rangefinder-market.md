<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Rangefinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Method Rangefinder</li><li>Pulse Method Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld Laser Rangefinder Market Types include Phase Method Rangefinder and Pulse Method Rangefinder. The Phase Method Rangefinder measures distance by comparing the phase difference between the emitted laser beam and the reflected beam. On the other hand, Pulse Method Rangefinder uses the time it takes for the laser pulse to travel to the target and back to calculate distance. Both types are popular choices for measuring distances accurately in various applications such as hunting, golfing, surveying, and construction.</p></p>
