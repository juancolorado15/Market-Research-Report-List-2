<p><p>Centrifugal pumps are classified into different types based on their flow patterns and design. Axial flow pumps are designed for high flow rates with low head, while Mixed or Radial Flow pumps are versatile and can handle a wide range of flow rates and heads. Peripheral pumps are suitable for high-pressure applications, and Jet pumps are used for deep well applications. Each type of centrifugal pump has specific advantages and applications based on the flow requirements and system design.</p></p>
