<p><p>Inspection drones come in two main types - fixed wing and rotor wing. Fixed wing drones are more suitable for covering large areas quickly, making them ideal for inspecting things like pipelines, power lines, and agriculture fields. On the other hand, rotor wing drones, such as quadcopters, are more maneuverable and can hover in place, making them better suited for inspecting structures like buildings, bridges, and cell towers. Both types of drones are commonly used in industries such as construction, utilities, and agriculture for efficient and cost-effective inspections.</p></p>
