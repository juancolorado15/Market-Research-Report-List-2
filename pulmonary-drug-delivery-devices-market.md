Dry powder inhalers deliver medication in a dry powdered form for easy inhalation, while metered dose inhalers deliver a measured dose of medication in an aerosol form. Nebulizers provide medication in a fine mist for inhalation. These devices offer varying benefits and are used based on individual patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
