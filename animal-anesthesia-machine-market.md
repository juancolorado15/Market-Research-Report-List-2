<p><ul><li>Air Anesthesia Machine</li><li>Once-Through Anesthesia Machine</li><li>Closed Loop Type Anesthesia Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Animal Anesthesia Machine market can be classified into three main types: Air Anesthesia Machine, Once-Through Anesthesia Machine, and Closed Loop Type Anesthesia Machine. Air Anesthesia Machine delivers anesthesia through air and is commonly used for small animals. Once-Through Anesthesia Machine delivers anesthesia in a continuous flow and is suitable for large animals. Closed Loop Type Anesthesia Machine recirculates anesthesia, providing precise control over the delivery of anesthetic gases, making it ideal for both small and large animal procedures.</p></p>
