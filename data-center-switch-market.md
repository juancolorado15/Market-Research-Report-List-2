<p><ul><li>Core Switches</li><li>Distribution Switches</li><li>Access Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center switches are categorized into three main types: Core Switches, Distribution Switches, and Access Switches. Core Switches are responsible for handling high volumes of data traffic within the data center network. Distribution Switches connect core switches to access switches, routing data traffic to the appropriate destination. Access Switches provide connectivity for end devices such as servers and storage devices. Each type of switch plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable communication within a data center network.</p></p>
