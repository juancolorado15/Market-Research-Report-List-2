<p><p>The high-end coffee machine market can be divided into two main types: drip coffee machines and capsule coffee machines. Drip coffee machines are traditional brewing devices that use ground coffee and a filter to brew a single pot of coffee. On the other hand, capsule coffee machines use pre-packaged coffee capsules to brew individual servings of coffee quickly and conveniently. Both types cater to different preferences, with drip machines offering a more hands-on brewing experience and capsule machines providing ease and convenience.</p></p>
