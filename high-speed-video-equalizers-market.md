<p><p>High Speed Video Equalizers are used in video production to modify the frequency response of a video signal. There are two main types of equalizers in the market: Semi-parametric Equalizers and Fully-parametric Equalizers. Semi-parametric equalizers allow for adjustment of specific frequency bands, while fully-parametric equalizers offer more precise control over frequency bands, including adjustable bandwidth and Q factors. Both types are essential tools for audio engineers and videographers to shape the sound and visuals of their productions to achieve the desired results.</p></p>
