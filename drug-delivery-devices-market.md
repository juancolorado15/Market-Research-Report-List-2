<p><strong>The Drug Delivery Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection type</li><li>Mucosal type</li><li>Implanted type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drug delivery devices market is divided into three main types: injection type, mucosal type, and implanted type. Injection type devices include syringes, pens, and pumps that deliver drugs directly into the bloodstream. Mucosal type devices deliver drugs through mucous membranes such as the nose, mouth, or lungs. Implanted type devices are surgically implanted under the skin to provide controlled and sustained release of medication. Each type offers unique benefits and is utilized based on the specific needs of the drug being delivered.</p></p>
