<p><p>The oil and gas pipeline leak detection equipment market includes various types of technologies such as flow meters, infra-red detection systems, and fiber optic sensors. Flow meters measure the flow rate of the pipeline to detect abnormal changes that may indicate a leak. Infra-red detection systems use infrared sensors to identify hydrocarbon gas leaks. Fiber optic sensors utilize fiber optic cables to detect changes in temperature or pressure within the pipeline that may indicate a leak. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of oil and gas pipelines.</p></p>
