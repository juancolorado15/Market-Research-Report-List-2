<p><p>High voltage ceramic capacitors are mainly classified into three types: X7R, C0G, and Others. X7R capacitors have a medium permittivity and are commonly used in noise suppression applications. C0G capacitors have a high permittivity and are preferred for high stability and low dielectric loss. Other types of ceramic capacitors include Y5V, Z5U, and NP0, each with specific characteristics and applications. Overall, the selection of a ceramic capacitor type depends on the specific requirements of the application in terms of stability, temperature coefficient, and capacitance range.</p></p>
