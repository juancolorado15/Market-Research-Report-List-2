<p><p>Osteochondral implants are used in orthopedic surgery to repair damaged cartilage and bone in joints. There are three main types of osteochondral implants in the market - allograft, screw, and plate. Allograft implants involve using donor tissue to replace damaged cartilage and bone. Screw implants involve securing the implant with screws, while plate implants involve using a plate to support and stabilize the implant. Each type of implant has its own unique advantages and applications in orthopedic surgery.</p></p>
