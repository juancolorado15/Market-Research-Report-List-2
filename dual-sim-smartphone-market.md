<p><p>Dual SIM smartphones are divided into two main types: Dual Standby mobile phones and Dual Standby Dual mobile phones. </p><p>In Dual Standby mobile phones, both SIM cards are active at the same time but only one can make or receive calls and texts at a time. On the other hand, in Dual Standby Dual mobile phones, both SIM cards can make and receive calls and texts simultaneously. These two types cater to different market segments with varying needs for managing multiple phone numbers and networks efficiently.</p></p>
