<p><p>Level Probes are instruments used to measure the level of a substance in a container. There are two main types of Level Probes in the market - Point Level Probes and Continuous Level Probes. Point Level Probes detect when a material reaches a certain level, triggering an alert or control action. Continuous Level Probes provide real-time, constant measurement of the material's level in the container. Both types of Level Probes have their own distinct advantages and are used in various industries for monitoring and control purposes.</p></p>
