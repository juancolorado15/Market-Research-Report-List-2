<p><p>Linear actuators are essential components in various industrial and commercial applications, with three main types dominating the market: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic actuators utilize compressed air to create linear motion, making them suitable for quick and precise movements. Electric actuators are powered by electricity and offer more flexibility in terms of control and speed adjustments. Hydraulic actuators, on the other hand, utilize fluid pressure for linear motion, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring high force. Each type has its unique advantages and is utilized in different industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
