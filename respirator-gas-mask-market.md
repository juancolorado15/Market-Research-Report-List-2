<p><p>Respirator Gas Masks are available in two main types: Full Face and Half Face. Full Face masks cover the entire face including the eyes, providing maximum protection for the wearer. Half Face masks cover only the nose and mouth, leaving the eyes unprotected. Full Face masks are typically used in situations where there is a higher risk of exposure to harmful gases, while Half Face masks are a more lightweight and comfortable option for less severe conditions. Both types serve the purpose of filtering and purifying the air for the wearer's safety.</p></p>
