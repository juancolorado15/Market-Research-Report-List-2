<p><p>Single Axis Solar PV Tracker market types include Horizontal Type and Vertical Type. Horizontal Type trackers rotate on a horizontal axis, following the path of the sun from east to west throughout the day. This design maximizes energy production by keeping the panels facing the sun. Vertical Type trackers rotate on a vertical axis, allowing them to track the sun's height in the sky as well as its east-west path. Both types offer increased efficiency compared to fixed solar panels.</p></p>
