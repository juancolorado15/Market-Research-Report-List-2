<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li><li>Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic position sensor market is segmented into two types: discrete ultrasonic position sensor and continuous ultrasonic position sensor. Discrete ultrasonic position sensors provide a single output signal when a target is within a specified range, whereas continuous ultrasonic position sensors offer a continuous output signal indicating the exact position of the target. Discrete sensors are commonly used in applications where presence or absence detection is required, while continuous sensors are used for precise distance and position measurement.</p></p>
