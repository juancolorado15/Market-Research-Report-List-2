<p><p>Air springs are categorized into three main types: Convoluted, Sleeves, and Others. Convoluted air springs have a bellows-shaped design, providing flexibility and support for heavy-duty applications. Sleeves air springs are cylindrical in shape and commonly used in vehicle suspension systems. The "Others" category includes various types such as rolling lobe air springs and reversible sleeve air springs. Each type offers specific advantages in terms of durability, load capacity, and performance, catering to different industrial and automotive needs.</p></p>
