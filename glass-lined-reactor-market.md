<p><strong>The Glass Lined Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AE Type</li><li>BE Type</li><li>CE Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass lined reactors are commonly categorized into three main types: AE Type, BE Type, and CE Type. AE Type reactors feature a two-part construction with a glass lining applied to the inside of a carbon steel shell. BE Type reactors have a two-part construction with a glass lining applied to the inside of a stainless steel shell. CE Type reactors have a one-piece construction with a glass lining directly on the inside of a carbon steel shell. Each type offers specific advantages and is used in various industries based on their unique features and capabilities.</p></p>
