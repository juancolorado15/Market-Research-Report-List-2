<p><p>Temporary bonding adhesives used in various industries like electronics, medical devices, and optics can be categorized based on the debonding method. Thermal slide-off debonding involves heating the adhesive to release the bonded materials. Mechanical debonding requires physical force to separate the materials. Laser debonding utilizes laser energy to break down the adhesive bond. Each type of debonding method offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the materials being bonded and the desired outcome of the bonding process.</p></p>
