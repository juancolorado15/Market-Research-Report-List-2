<p><p>Bluetooth trackers are electronic devices used to help locate lost items. There are different types of Bluetooth trackers available in the market, including Radio Frequency Trackers and BLE Bluetooth Tags. Radio Frequency Trackers operate using radio waves to track an item's location, while BLE Bluetooth Tags use Bluetooth Low Energy technology to connect to a smartphone and provide real-time location updates. Both types of trackers are popular among consumers looking to easily find misplaced items such as keys, wallets, and bags.</p></p>
