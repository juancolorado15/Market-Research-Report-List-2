<p><strong>The Fiber Raman Amplifier (FRA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lumped Type</li><li>Discrete Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Raman Amplifiers (FRA) are divided into two main types based on their structure: Lumped Type and Discrete Type. Lumped Type FRAs are compact devices where the Raman pump and the fiber are tightly integrated, providing efficient amplification in a small area. Discrete Type FRAs have separate Raman pumps and fibers, allowing for more flexibility in system design and easier maintenance. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the application, and their market presence reflects the diverse requirements of different industries.</p></p>
