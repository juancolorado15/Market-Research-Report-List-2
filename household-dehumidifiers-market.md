Portable dehumidifiers are smaller, more affordable units that can be easily moved around the home. Whole-home dehumidifiers are larger, more powerful systems that are typically installed directly into a home's HVAC system to regulate humidity levels throughout the entire house. Both types of dehumidifiers offer benefits in improving indoor air quality and preventing mold and mildew growth.</p></p>
