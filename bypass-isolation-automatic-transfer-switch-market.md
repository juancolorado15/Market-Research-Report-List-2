<p><p>Bypass isolation automatic transfer switches are crucial for ensuring uninterrupted power supply during switchovers. The two main market types for these switches are open transition and closed transition. In open transition, there is a brief interruption in power supply during the switch from one source to another. On the other hand, closed transition ensures a seamless transfer between power sources, eliminating any interruption. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of users looking for reliable and efficient power transfer solutions.</p></p>
