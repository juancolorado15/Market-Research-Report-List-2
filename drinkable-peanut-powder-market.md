<p><p>The drinkable peanut powder market can be classified into two types: soluble (powder) and insoluble (particle). Soluble peanut powder easily dissolves in liquid, making it ideal for creating smooth and creamy beverages. On the other hand, insoluble peanut powder consists of larger particles that do not fully dissolve in liquid, often used for adding texture and crunch to drinks. Both types offer unique benefits and applications, catering to different consumer preferences and product requirements in the beverage industry.</p></p>
