<p><strong>The Enterprise DECT Phones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SIP DECT</li><li>IP DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise DECT phones come in two main market types: SIP DECT and IP DECT. SIP DECT phones use the Session Initiation Protocol to communicate voice data over a network, allowing for integration with other SIP-based systems. On the other hand, IP DECT phones utilize Internet Protocol technology for voice communication, providing more flexibility and scalability. Both types offer reliable wireless communication within the enterprise environment, but IP DECT phones are especially favored for their ability to seamlessly connect to existing IP networks.</p></p>
