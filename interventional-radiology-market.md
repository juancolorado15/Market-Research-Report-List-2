<p><p>Interventional Radiology Market encompasses various types of imaging systems such as MRI System, Ultrasound Imaging System, CT Scanner, and Angiography System. MRI System uses magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images of internal organs. Ultrasound Imaging System uses sound waves to produce images of organs. CT Scanner utilizes X-rays to create cross-sectional images of the body. Angiography System visualizes blood vessels and organs by injecting contrast material. These technologies play a crucial role in guiding minimally invasive procedures for diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
