<p><p>Flexible PV cells are available in various types such as CIGS, a-Si, OPV, and others. CIGS cells are made of copper, indium, gallium, and selenium, offering high efficiency and durability. A-Si cells use non-crystalline silicon, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness. OPV cells use organic materials, allowing for lightweight and transparent designs. Other types include DSSC, Perovskite, and CdTe cells, each offering unique advantages such as high efficiency, low cost, and ease of manufacturing in the flexible PV cell market.</p></p>
