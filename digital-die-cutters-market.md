<p><p>Digital die cutters are machines that use advanced technology to cut materials with precision. There are two main types of digital die cutters on the market: blade cutting and laser cutting. Blade cutting uses a sharp, oscillating blade to cut through materials like paper, cardstock, and vinyl. On the other hand, laser cutting uses a high-powered laser to cut through a wider range of materials with more intricate designs. Both types of machines are popular in the crafting, manufacturing, and packaging industries for their efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
