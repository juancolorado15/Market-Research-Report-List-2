<p><strong>The Anesthesia Video Laryngoscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channelled Video Laryngoscopes</li><li>Un-Channelled Video Laryngoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Channelled Video Laryngoscopes have a built-in channel where the endotracheal tube can easily be inserted during intubation, allowing for a smoother and more efficient process. On the other hand, Un-Channelled Video Laryngoscopes do not have this channel, requiring a separate intubation technique. Both types offer clear and magnified views of the airway during intubation procedures, with Channelled Laryngoscopes providing added convenience for healthcare professionals. The choice between the two depends on the preference and experience of the medical practitioner.</p></p>
