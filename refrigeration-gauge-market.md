<p><p>Refrigeration gauges are instruments used to measure pressure and temperature in refrigeration systems. There are two main types of refrigeration gauges: analog and digital. Analog gauges use mechanical or hydraulic systems to display readings on a dial, while digital gauges use electronic sensors to provide precise numerical readings on a digital screen. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, with analog gauges being more traditional and durable, while digital gauges offer greater accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
