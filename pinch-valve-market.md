Manual pinch valves are operated manually, while air-operated pinch valves use compressed air to control the flow of materials. Hydraulic pinch valves utilize hydraulic pressure for operation, and electric pinch valves rely on an electric motor for control. Each type of pinch valve offers unique benefits and is used in a variety of industries for applications requiring shut-off or flow control.</p></p>
