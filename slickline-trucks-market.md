<p><strong>The Slickline Trucks Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Hole Applications</li><li>Cased Hole Applications</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slickline trucks are used in both open hole and cased hole applications within the oil and gas industry. Open hole applications involve running tools and equipment through the wellbore without the presence of casing. This is typically done for well evaluation, testing, and completion purposes. Cased hole applications, on the other hand, involve running tools and equipment through the wellbore that is already lined with casing. This is done for tasks such as well intervention, maintenance, and production optimization.</p></p>
