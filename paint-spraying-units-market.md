<p><p>Paint spraying units market is segmented into conventional sprayers, high volume low pressure sprayers, and others. Conventional sprayers use high pressure to atomize paint, resulting in a higher overspray and paint wastage. High volume low pressure sprayers, on the other hand, use a lower pressure which reduces overspray and paint wastage, making them more efficient and cost-effective. Other types of paint sprayers include airless sprayers and handheld sprayers which cater to specific painting needs and preferences.</p></p>
