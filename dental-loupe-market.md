<p><p>Dental loupes are magnification devices used by dentists and surgeons to improve precision and accuracy during procedures. There are two main types of dental loupes available: TTL loupes and flip-up loupes. TTL loupes are mounted directly onto the frame of glasses, providing a more stable and secure fit. On the other hand, flip-up loupes have a hinge mechanism that allows the user to easily flip the magnification lenses up and out of the way when not in use. Both types offer varying levels of magnification to meet the specific needs of dental professionals.</p></p>
