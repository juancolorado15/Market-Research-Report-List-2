<p><ul><li>IR-based Motion-controlled Remotes</li><li>RF-based Motion-controlled Remotes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of motion-controlled remotes on the market: IR-based and RF-based. IR-based remotes use infrared technology to communicate with devices by emitting signals that are picked up by sensors. They are commonly used for controlling devices within line of sight. RF-based remotes, on the other hand, use radio frequency signals to communicate with devices, allowing for a longer range and the ability to control devices even when they are not in direct line of sight.</p></p>
