<p><strong>The Spray Drying Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray drying equipment market can be categorized into three types: single stage, two-stage, and multi-stage systems. Single stage systems involve a single drying chamber where the liquid feed is sprayed and dried in one process. Two-stage systems have a separate drying chamber and drying chamber where the feed is initially pre-dried before entering the main drying chamber. Multi-stage systems have multiple drying chambers for sequential drying stages, allowing for precise control over the drying process and improved product quality.</p></p>
