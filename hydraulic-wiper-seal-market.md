<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic wiper seals are classified into two main types based on their functionality: single-acting and double-acting seals. Single-acting seals are designed to prevent dust, dirt, and debris from entering the hydraulic system in one direction, typically during the retraction stroke. On the other hand, double-acting seals provide sealing in both directions, ensuring maximum protection against contaminants. Both types of seals are crucial in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of hydraulic systems in various industries.</p></p>
