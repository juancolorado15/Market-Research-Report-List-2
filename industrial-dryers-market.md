<p><p>Industrial dryers are categorized into three main types, Direct Dryers, Indirect Dryers, and Specialty Dryers. Direct dryers use direct contact between the material and the drying medium to remove moisture. Indirect dryers utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat to the material. Specialty dryers are designed for specific applications, such as freeze drying or vacuum drying. Each type of dryer has its own advantages and is suited for different industries and processes.</p></p>
