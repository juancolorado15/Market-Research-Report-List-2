<p><p>Drive shafts are essential components in vehicles, transferring power from the engine to the wheels. There are two main types of drive shafts in the market: hollow and rigid. Hollow drive shafts are lighter and more flexible, making them ideal for vehicles with limited space. Rigid drive shafts are more durable and provide better power transmission, suitable for heavy-duty vehicles with high torque requirements. Both types cater to different needs within the automotive industry, offering options for various vehicle designs and applications.</p></p>
