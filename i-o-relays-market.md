<p><p>I & O relays are critical components in various electronic devices. Electromagnetic relays use electromagnets to mechanically switch the contacts, while solid state relays use semiconductor devices to switch the contacts. Electromagnetic relays are known for their durability and reliability, making them suitable for high current applications. On the other hand, solid state relays offer faster switching speeds and longer lifespan. Both types of relays cater to different market needs based on factors such as cost, performance, and application requirements.</p></p>
