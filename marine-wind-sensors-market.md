<p><p>Marine wind sensors are typically classified into three main types: Ultrasonic Type, Mechanical Type, and Others. Ultrasonic Type sensors use sound waves to measure wind speed and direction, providing accurate and reliable data. Mechanical Type sensors use traditional rotating cups or vanes to measure wind speed, while Others category includes innovative sensor technologies such as lidar or laser-based systems. Each type of sensor has its own unique advantages and applications in the marine industry, catering to a wide range of user needs.</p></p>
