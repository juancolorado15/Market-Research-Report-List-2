<p><strong>The Lithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mask Aligner</li><li>Laser Direct Imaging</li><li>Projection</li><li>Laser Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithography equipment market consists of different types of technologies used for patterning in semiconductor manufacturing. Mask aligner uses masks to transfer patterns onto substrates. Laser direct imaging involves using lasers to directly write patterns onto substrates. Projection lithography uses lenses to project patterns onto substrates. Laser ablation involves using lasers to remove material from substrates. Each of these technologies plays a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by enabling the precise patterning required for integrated circuits and other electronic devices.</p></p>
