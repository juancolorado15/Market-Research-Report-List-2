<p><p>Roll Bond Evaporator Market is divided into two types: Double Side and Single Side. Double Side Roll Bond Evaporators have two layers of bonded aluminum, making them more efficient in heat transfer and suitable for high-performance applications. On the other hand, Single Side Roll Bond Evaporators have only one layer of aluminum, making them more cost-effective and suitable for less-demanding cooling requirements. Both types are widely used in refrigeration and air conditioning systems for their compact design and effective heat transfer capabilities.</p></p>
